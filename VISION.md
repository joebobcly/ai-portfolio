# Portfolio Vision

## Why I Am Building This

AI is rapidly changing how growth and demand generation teams research markets, develop campaigns, create content, analyze performance, and optimize customer experiences.

I am building practical AI systems that demonstrate how experienced marketing leadership can be combined with modern AI capabilities.

## Professional Positioning

I am a growth marketing leader who builds AI-powered systems that help marketing teams move faster, make better decisions, and scale revenue more efficiently.

## Intended Audience

This portfolio is designed for:

- Hiring managers
- Marketing executives
- Recruiters
- Growth and demand generation leaders
- Potential collaborators
- Companies evaluating practical applications of AI in marketing

## What Each Project Should Demonstrate

Each portfolio project should demonstrate at least one of the following:

- Strong marketing judgment
- Practical application of AI
- Workflow automation
- Clear business value
- Thoughtful system design
- Responsible use of data
- Measurable improvement in speed, quality, or decision-making

## Initial Project

The first project will be an AI Landing Page Auditor.

The system will evaluate a landing page and provide structured recommendations covering:

- Messaging
- Value proposition
- Conversion friction
- Calls to action
- Page structure
- Audience alignment
- Search optimization
- Suggested copy improvements
